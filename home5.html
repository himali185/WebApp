About Us
We, at EventBear are pioneering in the WEB3 space with utility NFT solutions to tackle real world problems pertaining to Event Booking and Ticketingoperations.

From music festivals, marathons, conferences, community rallies, and fundraisers, to gaming competitions and air guitar contests. Our mission is to bring the world together through live experiences and more!
